A Comprehensive Overview

Let’s navigate the IT Landscape with BCS! 2014 is the right year to start! With BCS qualifications, you're not just earning a certificate; you're investing in your future. To begin an explicit career with BCS you will need the help of this blog article. Continue reading for further knowledge.

The British Computer Society (BCS) is an important organisation in the UK for technology. This group started in 1957. They have been important in shaping computing and technology in the UK and other places. The BCS wants to:

  • Advance knowledge
  • Promote professionalism
  • Support IT professionals

They have helped the tech industry grow. This introduction gives an overview of the BCS.

Digital Literacy and Professionalism

BCS is one of the most important organisations in the UK when it comes to teaching people how to use technology and keeping professional standards high. It helps people learn how to use technology by taking part in educational programmes that help people at all stages of schooling get better at IT. The society aims to improve the IT workforce by offering a wide range of:

  • Certifications
  • Qualifications
  • Opportunities for professional growth

These courses:

  • Not only help IT workers improve their skills
  • But they also set industry-wide standards for professionalism and skill

Through its Code of Conduct, BCS makes sure that its members work in a way that is:

  • Honest
  • Responsible
  • Professional

Adaptations are Important in Project Management  

Change is the only thing that remains the same in the world, and project management is no different. As the UK faces challenges it has never seen before, like figuring out how to live in a post-Brexit world and keeping up with the fast pace of technological change, it is important to be able to adapt.  

This spirit of change is captured in the 7th Edition of PRINCE2.   

Not only is it important to stay current, but it's also important to lead the way and set new standards for project management excellence. This release shows that we will always do our best to give workers in the UK the best tools and methods.   

Therefore, the 7th Edition is made for you, with the backbone of the UK's project management community in mind. Whether you're an experienced PRINCE2 practitioner or someone who wants to start their project management journey you will find this helpful.   

Stay tuned as we go into more detail about how the 7th Edition of PRINCE2 works.

Explaining the structure of the PRINCE2® 7th Edition 

The PRINCE2 design has always been recognised for being clear, well-organised, and accurate. As we move through the 7th Edition, these core strengths not only stay the same, but they are also brought out even more.  

How the Methodical Chapters are Put Together 

The 7th Edition is a great example of how to organise things in a methodical way. It was carefully made to make sure that each part flows into the next so that readers can learn in an easy manner.

This layout helps both newcomers and experienced practitioners because it gives them a structured way to learn and makes it easy to find specific parts. The 7th Edition of PRINCE2 starts with a thorough opening that sets the stage for what's to come.

The next step is an in-depth look at PRINCE2's core pillars:

  • The principles, which describe the overarching philosophies;
  • The practises (formerly called themes), which describe the specific areas of project management; and
  • The processes, which describe the lifecycle and steps of a project from start to finish.

The new cross-referencing system is better. The new cross-referencing method is one of the best new features in the 7th Edition. Understanding that different parts of project management work together, this method makes it easy for readers to see how different sections fit together.

Whether you want to know how a specific practise relates to a principle or how processes relate to practises, the cross-references will help you find your way and make sure you understand the PRINCE2 method as a whole.In simple terms, the PRINCE2 7th Edition offers a structured, clear, and connected learning experience that will make the vast area of project management easier to navigate than ever before.

  1. Better cross-referencing

    2. Imagine this as a map in a large book. If you're reading about one thing and want to see how it relates to something else, this function makes it easy to switch between them. It's like being able to cut corners! 

  2. Global Perspectives

    3. People from all over the world contributed thoughts to this new version. So, it should make sense and help you no matter where you are.

  3. Updated Core Ideas

    4. PRINCE2 is known for some of its main ideas. They took a new look at these ideas to make them even better. 

  4. Simpler Processes and New "Practices"

    5. They have made things easier and changed the name of a part. "Practises" are now what "themes" used to be. It's like changing a book chapter to tell you more about what's in it.  

  5. Fixing Project Problems

    6. They have made it easier to deal with problems that come up during a project. 

  6. New Digital Stuff

    7. They have added fun online tools and content that you can connect with. Think of it like getting extra features for your favourite online game.  

  7. Paying attention to people skills

    8. Not only does it matter how well you do your job, but also how well you work with others. They are paying more attention to skills like working as a team and talking with others.   

  8. Working well with other framework

    9. PRINCE2 has created links with other well-known frameworks like Agile, Lean, and ITIL because it knows there are many ways to handle projects. This integration makes sure that project managers can move quickly through different environments and get the most out of each way.  

The 7th edition of PRINCE2 brought about some changes. There are some new things you should know if you're thinking about getting a PRINCE2 certification.  

  1. New Training Materials 

    2. PRINCE2's training materials have been updated, just like when your school textbooks get new versions. This means that you'll learn the newest and best things!  

  2. What's the exam like? 

    3. Imagine you were taking an exam in which they told you a story or situation and then asked you questions about it. Hence, that's a case. In order to improve the exam, PRINCE2 has added or changed some of these stories.  

  3. Format of the Exam  

    4. For those who like specifics, there is a 60-minute test called "Foundation" that has 60 multiple-choice questions. To pass, you need to get 60% right. The practitioner test takes 150 minutes and has 68 multiple-choice questions. You also need a 60% score to pass. 

  1. Sustainability & ethics in project management. 

    2. Sustainability, which means taking care of our planet, and ethics are now talked about more in PRINCE2. It follows that when people are in charge of projects, they will think about how to be kind to our world and act fairly. 

  2. The concept of tailoring for unique project needs. 

    3. Just as we sometimes change a recipe to suit our tastes, PRINCE2 now puts more emphasis on "tailoring." This means that the project plan needs to be changed so that it fits exactly what is needed.

  3. Visual elements and design updates. 

    4. More pictures, sketches, and designs have been added to the new PRINCE2. This makes it easy to understand and looks a lot better. 

  4. Keeping up with the digital age 

    5. Because there is so much technology around us, PRINCE2 now gives tools and tips on how to handle digital things and a lot of data. It's like giving project managers a new set of high-tech tools to add to their toolbox! 

The UK and the rest of the world are changing quickly, making it clear that we must not only keep up but also lead the way.

The PRINCE2 7th Edition is not just an update; it is also a big step forward. It takes into account the needs of today, looks ahead to the problems of tomorrow, and gives people both technical and personal tools to deal with them. It keeps the core values and strengths of its predecessors but adds new ideas, deeper links, and a good grasp of the digital age we live in now.

As project managers or people who want to become professionals, now is the right time to get on board with this modern view of project management.

Frequently asked questions

If you're stepping into PRINCE2 without prior qualifications, it's advisable to start with the PRINCE2 7th Edition Foundation and Practitioner Courses

For holders of the PRINCE2 6th Edition Practitioner certification wanting to transition to the 7th Edition, beginning with the 7th Edition Foundation course is recommended.

Foundation Level - The 7th Edition retains the Foundation level examination format. Yet, the passing criteria have been elevated from 55% to 60%, requiring 36 right answers. Practitioner Level - The Practitioner exam for the 7th Edition has expanded to 70 questions, a slight increase from 68 in the previous version. While the exam duration remains at 2.5 hours, the passing score is now 60%. This exam will derive its questions from one of the four new manual scenarios.

The PRINCE2® Foundation (7th Edition) training course will be available starting from September 2023

Foundation Level - No prior qualifications are necessary for the 7th Edition Foundation course.
